Overview

Purpose

My purpose for my wed site is to make people happy. I want people who might be feeling down or stressed, to come to my site and find a quote that will alleviate their mood.

Audience

My audience is all ages. Anyone who is looking for a quote to lighten up their day. The people who will come to my web site will be people that love quotes or are wanting to find a quote for any purpose.
